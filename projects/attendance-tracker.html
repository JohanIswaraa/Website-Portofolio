Attendance Tracker App

Java Kotlin Front End Mobile App

Project Overview

NeoEmployee is a comprehensive Android mobile application designed to streamline every aspect of employee management for distributed teams. By unifying attendance tracking, task and project management, meeting scheduling, internal communications, and logistics into a single, intuitive platform, NeoEmployee empowers organizations to optimize workflows, reduce administrative overhead, and keep everyone connected—no matter where they’re located.

Key Features

  • Smart Attendance System: GPS-verified check-in/check-out with geofencing across multiple office locations, automated overtime calculation, and real-time reporting.
  • Task & Project Management: Create, assign, and track tasks (and subtasks) with due dates, priorities, Kanban and calendar views, plus productivity dashboards.
  • Meeting Scheduler: Room booking and calendar sync (Google/Outlook), RSVP tracking, automated reminders, and support for recurring and ad-hoc meetings.
  • Company News Hub: Publish rich-media announcements, enable likes/comments, and track engagement in a searchable, filterable archive.
  • Resources & Benefits Portal: Centralized access to HR documents, payroll and benefits details, leave-request workflows, and integrated approval tracking.
  • Inter-Office Chat: 1:1 and group messaging with file sharing, status indicators tied to attendance, and searchable message history to replace email chains.
  • Pick-Delivery-Order System: Internal logistics module that matches delivery requests to available staff, provides real-time order tracking, secure in-app payments, and post-delivery ratings.

Technical Details

  • Frontend: Android (Kotlin, MVVM) targeting API 26–34; Material Design components & Firebase real-time syncing.
  • Backend: Laravel (PHP) REST API; Firebase Firestore (data), Auth (login) & Messaging (push notifications).
  • DevOps & CI: GitHub Actions for CI/CD; separate development, staging, and production environments.
  • Architecture & Methodology: Two-tier client/server design with JWT authentication; Agile development with two-week sprints, continuous integration, and regular sprint reviews.

Results & Impact

  • 40% reduction in administrative time spent on attendance management.
  • 30% improvement in meeting attendance rates.
  • Cross-departmental email volume reduced by over 50%.
  • Positive pilot-user feedback leading to a full company-wide rollout.
